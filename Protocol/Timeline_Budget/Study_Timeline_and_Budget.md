# Study Timeline and Budget - Allergy Epidemiology Study

**Study Title:** Epidemiological Study on Allergy in SIMS&RH Tumkur, Karnataka

**Total Duration:** 16 months

**Total Budget:** ₹25,00,000 (approximately $30,000 USD)

---

## 1. Detailed Study Timeline

### Phase 1: Protocol Development and Ethical Approval (Months 1-3)

#### Month 1: Protocol Development
- **Week 1-2:** Literature review and protocol drafting
- **Week 3-4:** Questionnaire development and pilot testing
- **Deliverables:** Draft protocol, questionnaires, data collection forms

#### Month 2: Ethical and Administrative Preparations
- **Week 1-2:** Ethics committee submission preparation
- **Week 3-4:** Regulatory approvals and permissions
- **Deliverables:** Ethics submission, regulatory approvals

#### Month 3: Training and Pilot Study
- **Week 1-2:** Staff recruitment and training
- **Week 3-4:** Pilot study in small sample
- **Deliverables:** Trained staff, pilot study report

**Milestones:** Ethics approval, final protocol, trained team

### Phase 2: Main Data Collection (Months 4-10)

#### Month 4: Final Preparations
- Equipment procurement and calibration
- Final training and standardization
- Community sensitization

#### Months 5-10: Field Data Collection (6 months)
- **Urban areas:** Months 5-7
- **Rural areas:** Months 7-9
- **Hospital-based recruitment:** Ongoing throughout
- **Laboratory sample collection:** Parallel to clinical data

**Weekly targets:** 20-25 participants per week
**Quality monitoring:** Weekly supervision visits

**Milestones:** Complete recruitment, data collection closure

### Phase 3: Laboratory Analysis (Months 8-10)
- **Microbiome analysis:** Months 8-9
- **Microplastic analysis:** Months 9-10
- **Allergen testing:** Ongoing

### Phase 4: Data Analysis and Report Writing (Months 11-14)

#### Month 11: Data Management
- Data entry and cleaning
- Database finalization

#### Months 12-13: Analysis
- Descriptive analysis
- Bivariate and multivariate analysis
- Microbiome and spatial analysis

#### Month 14: Report Writing
- Draft reports and manuscripts
- Results validation

### Phase 5: Dissemination (Months 15-16)
- Publications and presentations
- Policy recommendations
- Community feedback
