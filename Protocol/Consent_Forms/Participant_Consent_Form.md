## Participant Information Sheet

### Introduction
We invite you to participate in a research study about allergies in our community. This study is being conducted by doctors and researchers from SIMS&RH to understand how common allergies are in Tumkur and what factors might cause them.

### Why is this study being done?
Allergies are becoming more common worldwide, but we don't have enough information about allergies in India, especially in places like Tumkur. This study will help us understand:
- How many people have allergies
- What causes allergies
- How allergies affect daily life
- What people think about allergy treatments

### Who can participate?
- People aged 5-65 years
- Residents of Tumkur district for at least 6 months
- People who are willing to answer questions and have a simple medical check-up

### What will happen if you agree to participate?
If you agree to participate, we will:
1. **Ask you questions** (about 30-45 minutes) about your health, lifestyle, and environment
2. **Do a simple medical check-up** (about 20-30 minutes) including:
   - Measuring height, weight, and blood pressure
   - Listening to your breathing
   - Checking your nose, throat, and skin
   - A simple allergy skin test (like a mosquito bite test)
3. **Collect some samples** (optional, about 10 minutes):
   - A small stool sample for gut bacteria study
   - Water or dust samples from your home

### Will it hurt?
- The questions and most check-ups will not hurt
- The allergy skin test might feel like a mosquito bite for a few minutes
- We have medicines ready if you have any allergic reaction

### How much time will it take?
- First visit: About 1-2 hours
- You may be asked to come back if needed
- Sample collection can be done at home

### What are the benefits?
- You will get a free health check-up
- You will learn about your allergies and how to manage them
- The study will help improve allergy care in our community
- You will contribute to medical knowledge

### What are the risks?
- Very small risk of allergic reaction to the skin test
- Some questions might make you uncomfortable
- Your personal information will be kept confidential

### Will you get paid?
- No payment for participating
- Travel costs will be covered if needed
- Free medical care if you have any problems from the study

### Will your information be kept private?
- Yes, completely confidential
- Your name will not be used in reports
- Only study doctors will see your information
- Information will be stored securely

### What if you change your mind?
- You can stop at any time, no questions asked
- Your health care will not be affected
- You can ask us to destroy your samples
